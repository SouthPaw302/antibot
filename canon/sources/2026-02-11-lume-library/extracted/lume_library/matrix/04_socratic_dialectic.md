    # Socratic Dialectic Sessions

    ## Purpose

This section encodes a foundational method: inquiry through definitions, counterexamples, and clarification. It functions as a tool to stabilize your larger metaphysical architectures.

## Core Claims

- **Clarity is power:** definitions control the argument.
- **Belief ≠ knowledge:** claims require grounding and internal consistency.
- **Contradiction is diagnostic:** incoherence indicates either wrong premises or wrong terms.

## Canonical Motifs

- “What do you mean by X?”
- Decomposing moral terms (good/evil/authority/freedom)
- Refusing rhetorical shortcuts
